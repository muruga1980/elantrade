About ÉLAN

ÉLAN TRADELINKS LLP is a dynamic, Dubai-based petrochemical trading and supply company specializing in the sourcing, marketing, and distribution of premium petroleum and hydrocarbon products.

We focus strongly on quality, compliance, and timely delivery, serving industrial clients, traders, and end-users across regional and international markets. With deep industry knowledge and a reliable supplier network, we provide customized solutions that meet diverse operational and commercial requirements.

We believe in building sustainable business relationships driven by transparency, accountability, and operational excellence. Our management approach focuses on long-term partnerships rather than short-term transactions, ensuring consistent value creation for customers, suppliers, and stakeholders.

Our Services

End-to-End Petrochemical Trading & Supply Solutions

verified Quality Assurance

All products supported by Certificate of Analysis (COA) and third-party laboratory testing (SGS/Intertek). Standard test parameters include viscosity, density, flash point, sulphur content, water content, and ash content analysis.

sync_alt International & Domestic Trading

Bulk supply and contract sales with international & domestic trading expertise. Competitive pricing with transparent documentation and direct relationships with refineries and major suppliers.

description Compliance & Documentation

Trade compliance support with export-import regulatory guidance. Environmental and handling regulations expertise with proper labeling, MSDS, and safety documentation. Accurate Bills of Lading, invoices, and packing lists.

local_shipping Logistics & Shipping Coordination

ISO tank, flexi tank, and bulk vessel shipments with containerized cargo solutions. Strong coordination with shipping lines, terminals, and clearing agents with real-time shipment tracking.

science Quality Inspection & Lab Testing

Quality inspection coordination with third-party lab testing (SGS, Intertek, equivalent). COA generation, complete technical documentation support, and spec sheet provision for all products.

hub Reliable Sourcing Network

Direct relationships with refineries and producers ensuring consistent supply solutions. Customized sourcing by requirement with global network coverage across key markets.
