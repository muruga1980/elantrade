Regional Expertise

Deep market knowledge across key regions

Middle East

As our primary sourcing hub, the Middle East provides direct access to major refineries and petrochemical producers. We maintain strong relationships with suppliers across the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Oman, and Qatar.

This strategic positioning allows us to source high-quality products at competitive prices with reliable supply chains and efficient logistics coordination through major ports including Jebel Ali, Port of Jeddah, and Port of Dammam.

Africa

Our expanding distribution network across Africa serves growing industrial demand in East Africa (Kenya, Tanzania, Ethiopia), West Africa (Nigeria, Ghana, Senegal), and North Africa (Egypt, Morocco, Algeria).

We understand the unique logistics challenges of African markets and have developed tailored solutions for port handling, inland transportation, and compliance with regional regulations.

South Asia

Strategic partnerships with industrial users and traders in India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, and Sri Lanka form a cornerstone of our business. The region's rapidly growing manufacturing sector drives consistent demand for our product portfolio.

We offer flexible delivery options including major Indian ports (Mumbai, Chennai, Mundra), Pakistani ports (Karachi, Port Qasim), and Chittagong in Bangladesh.

Southeast Asia

A key growth market for petrochemical derivatives and specialty products. We serve industrial clients in Indonesia, Malaysia, Thailand, Vietnam, and the Philippines with customized supply solutions.

Our regional expertise covers the specialized requirements of Southeast Asian manufacturing, from palm oil processing chemicals to industrial solvents and fuel products.
