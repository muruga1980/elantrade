Our Services

End-to-End Petrochemical Trading & Supply Solutions

verified Quality Assurance

All products supported by Certificate of Analysis (COA) and third-party laboratory testing (SGS/Intertek). Standard test parameters include viscosity, density, flash point, sulphur content, water content, and ash content analysis. We ensure every shipment meets international standards before delivery.

sync_alt International & Domestic Trading

Bulk supply and contract sales with international & domestic trading expertise. Competitive pricing with transparent documentation and direct relationships with refineries and major suppliers. We handle complex multi-country transactions with ease and regulatory compliance.

description Compliance & Documentation

Trade compliance support with export-import regulatory guidance. Environmental and handling regulations expertise with proper labeling, MSDS, and safety documentation. Accurate Bills of Lading, invoices, and packing lists ensure smooth customs clearance.

local_shipping Logistics & Shipping Coordination

ISO tank, flexi tank, and bulk vessel shipments with containerized cargo solutions. Strong coordination with shipping lines, terminals, and clearing agents with real-time shipment tracking. We manage end-to-end logistics from supplier to your doorstep.

science Quality Inspection & Lab Testing

Quality inspection coordination with third-party lab testing (SGS, Intertek, equivalent). COA generation, complete technical documentation support, and spec sheet provision for all products. Independent verification at loading and discharge ports.

hub Reliable Sourcing Network

Direct relationships with refineries and producers ensuring consistent supply solutions. Customized sourcing by requirement with global network coverage across key markets. We leverage our network to secure competitive pricing and reliable delivery schedules.
